Research interests

My research interests are composed by topics in signal processing and their intersection with applied mathematics. I aim to make contributions such as mathematical statistics, inverse problems and functional optimization without loosing sight of their engineering applications. Currently, my focus is on inverse problems and their application to biomedical imaging. Previously, I have been involved in projects concerning biomedical imaging, over-the-air clock synchronization, data quantization, and the foundations of privacy-preserving data analysis.
